About IC3IOT2026

The International Conference on Computing, Communication, and IoT (IC3IOT2026) is a premier forum for researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to exchange insights and innovations in computing, communication, and the Internet of Things.

Our Mission

This conference aims to foster collaboration and address the challenges and opportunities in these rapidly evolving fields. We bring together the brightest minds to share knowledge, spark new ideas, and shape the future of technology.

Conference Focus

IC3IOT2026 will focus on the latest advancements in computing paradigms, communication technologies, and IoT applications. Key areas include, but are not limited to:

  • ▸
    Computing: Cloud computing, edge computing, fog computing, high-performance computing, quantum computing, and distributed systems.
  • ▸
    Communication: 5G/6G networks, wireless communication, satellite communication, optical communication, and network security.
  • ▸
    IoT: IoT architectures, IoT security, IoT applications in smart cities, healthcare, agriculture, and industrial IoT.

Organizers

IC3IOT2026 is organized by the Department of Electronics and Communication (ECE) of Sri Sairam Engineering College in collaboration with leading academic institutions and industry partners. The organizing committee comprises renowned experts in computing, communication, and IoT, ensuring a high-quality and impactful conference.
